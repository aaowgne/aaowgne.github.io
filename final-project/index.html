Our Commitment

A Place For Temple Patrons To Relax and Enjoy Their Temple Experience

The Temple Inn & Suites is a hotel chain headquartered in Bethesda, MD that builds and manages specialized hotel accommodations near temple locations of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints around the world. The owner-operated company caters to temple workers, patrons, and their families by providing specialized services and accommodations that meet the needs of those patrons. The hotel accommodations and services are available to anyone willing to abide by the terms and policies outlined by the company. Accommodations can be made for events such as sealings, weddings, receptions, and youth trips, along with providing a place to live for service missionaries working at the temple.
