Wiener Dogs Search For Treasure


Two brown wiener dogs spent the day searching for the lost treasure of Fish Haven. Sources confirm that the bonded pair set out early in the morning to search several fields in town today. With all of the speculation that there could be buried treasure, people have resorted to any means possible to attempt to locate the riches. When asked to comment on the situation, Mayor Jones released the following statement: "There have been many attempts to locate the treasure. While our preferred method for searching would be hairless cats, we welcome the attempt being made here today." When pressed for further confirmation that the treasure had been found, he replied: "We cannot confirm or deny the rumors". A message that was left with the Chief of Police has gone unanswered at this point.
