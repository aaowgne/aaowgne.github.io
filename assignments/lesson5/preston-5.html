Snowfall Makes for Treacherous Driving

Conditions proved to be challenging for drivers yesterday as the storm dropped anywhere from 6-14 inches of fresh snow throughout the state.
Idaho State Police reported 345 crashes before 5 PM and that total is sure to increase as snowfall continued into the evening.
Snow plows were on the roads for over 24 hours, and while they are working hard to clear every road, it may take several days to reach every side street.
Thankfully there is a positive outlook concerning the weather. Sunny skies, along with temperatures that will reach above freezing are in store for the next five days.
